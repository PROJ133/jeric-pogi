About Us

THE MAN WOMAN BEHIND IT

Misffa Elaine Guinto 34-year-old seafarer from Silang, Cavite. Her entrepreneurial venture is the result of her lasagna fixation. It all begins when the government announces a pandemic in 2020 when COVID-19 attacks the Philippines. She considered starting a lasagna business since she was desperate to have a source of income because there were no jobs due to the pandemic. She conducts research to enhance the flavor and add something special. After she finished it, her friends sampled it and declared it to be wonderful. As a result, she spread the word about the business, which continued to expand not only in Cavite but also to Nueva Ecija.
